## Goal

Deliver `demos/16-agent-workflow` and a one-command acceptance suite: an unhealthy deployment triggers a workflow that collects diagnostics, runs the investigator agent, requests human approval, and — on approval — rolls back the deployment and stores a final report. Restarting the service mid-run resumes without repeating steps. This is the epic acceptance gate.

## Why this step exists

The epic is done only when durability, agent steps, approval, and compensation are proven together end-to-end (`specs.md` Step 16 acceptance).
